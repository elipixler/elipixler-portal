# Eli Pixler Studios — Client Portal
## How to use

### FILES IN THIS FOLDER
- admin.html  → YOUR page (password protected, never share this link)
- client.html → Your brides' page (they access via a unique link)

---

### STEP 1 — Host on Netlify (free, 5 min)
1. Go to netlify.com and sign up for a free account
2. Click "Add new site" → "Deploy manually"
3. Drag and drop this ENTIRE FOLDER onto the page
4. Netlify gives you a URL like: https://yoursite.netlify.app
5. You can change the name to something like: https://elipixler.netlify.app

---

### STEP 2 — Log into your admin
1. Go to: https://yoursite.netlify.app/admin.html
2. Password is: elipixler2025
   (To change it, open admin.html and find: const ADMIN_PASSWORD = 'elipixler2025')

---

### STEP 3 — Create a client portal
1. In the admin, type the bride's name, wedding date, and a slug
   - Slug = the URL nickname, e.g. "sarah-johnson" → yoursite.netlify.app/client.html?bride=sarah-johnson
2. Hit "Create Portal"
3. Click "Copy Link" to get her unique URL
4. Text or email that link to your bride!

---

### STEP 4 — Add videos to a portal
Currently videos are added via the + button on the client page itself.
A future version can add video upload directly from the admin panel.

---

### IMPORTANT NOTE
Videos uploaded via the browser are session-only (they disappear on refresh).
For permanent video delivery, the best free option is:
- Upload videos to Google Drive or Dropbox
- Make them "Anyone with link can view"
- Paste the direct video URL into the portal

We can build that into the admin panel as a next step!
